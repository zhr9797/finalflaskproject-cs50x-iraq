The using

of technology was not restricted to ages 18 and over, but included young ages ranging from 7 to 14 years, as this age group uses smart devices to install electronic games and watch animated films. The period it takes for children to use smart devices according to a statistic that was prepared by the coding for kids initiative and published on social networking sites. The largest percentage ranged from 4 to 5 hours per day.

In 2014,

the United States of America launched

Code.org to invest children's time in using technology by teaching them to code through entertainment and education. The MIT team launched the Scratch platform to teach children to program as well.Through this platform, children can design electronic stories and games as well.


How was

the start of our initiative

In 2012, during my masters studies in Turkey, my wife and I were 5 years old, my son Mustafa was related to computers. It takes 3 hours to watch animated films every day, and while browsing the Internet, how can I invest this time with children? I found the sites mentioned above and others .. From here, the coding for kids initiative was launched to teach youngsters to code using free open source platforms such as Scratch Code.org Developers Google Trinket Python MakeCode ... Where the first free workshop was organized in Baghdad in cooperation with the Syndicate of Engineers. After a while, workshops were organized in different areas in Baghdad. The main objective of the initiative is to add programming and robotics skills to children, as programming increases their logical thinking. Steve Jobs, founder of Apple, focused on important points in teaching children to code at an early age, namely Why kids should learn to code I believe kids should learn to code, they should start at an early age, and they should develop their skills as far as they can while they are at school. A number of reasons have been put forth for why kids should learn to code. 1. Coding helps kids develop logical thinking and problem-solving skills. 2. Coding teaches kids how to work well in teams. The workshops were not limited to Baghdad, but included the governorates as well, where events and workshops were organized in the governorates (Basra, Diyala, Anbar / Haditha, Wasit / Essaouira, Babil, Karbala, Najaf, and Muthanna). Cooperation with universities and youth and sports directorates in those governorates is done to provide Halls, computers and data show. After educating on the importance of programming for children, the second step was taken, which is to train a group of young people in different governorates in order to continue the development of children continuously. A YouTube channel has been created to teach children to code to reach a large number of parents, and they, in turn, teach their children to code. After a long way to go, a festival called Scratch Baghdad was organized in conjunction with Scratch Day, which is an international day celebrated by the mit team every year to train the largest number of youngsters. The date of the festival is 04/23/2019, as it was organized in cooperation with The Station The MoonHack Baghdad Festival was also organized in conjunction with MoonHack Day International on 6/23/2019, which is organized by Code Club UK and its goal is to train young people in programming. Why is this day, the day that humankind arrived in gambling, where young people were able to program a game that is access Moon spacecraft. The first training course of its kind in Iraq was organized under the name Make Baghdad Digital, which is training young people on the basics of electronics using a microbit, a device manufactured by Microsoft and in cooperation with the BBC, where the name Make it Digital was launched by the BBC channel These days, we are in the process of launching the Little Programmer Festival, which will be in cooperation with the directorates of education, which will consist of training a group of students on programming and dividing them into groups and creating projects by them. The curriculum followed by our initiative and it is available from the platforms we use such as Scratch Platform Code.org Code Club
